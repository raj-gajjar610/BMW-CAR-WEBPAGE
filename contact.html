Contact Us

Your BMW Dealership

Your local BMW dealership is the first point of contact for immediate assistance—service requests, general inquiries, or ownership issues. You’ll receive a unique case reference number to track your request. A dedicated advisor will oversee progress and support resolution.

BMW India Customer Interaction Centre

For further assistance, reach out to:

  • Phone: 1800 102 2269 (Mon–Sat, 09:00–18:30)
  • Email: contact.india@bmw.in

When contacting, please provide:

  • Full name & contact details
  • Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)
  • Description of the issue (with images/videos if available)
  • Desired resolution and any supporting documents (e.g., service records)

Accident & Breakdown Assistance (24×7)

  • Helpline: 1800 103 2211 (Available 24×7)

Grievance Redressal Officer

If your issue remains unresolved, escalate to the Grievance Redressal Officer, who will conduct an independent review to ensure fair resolution.
