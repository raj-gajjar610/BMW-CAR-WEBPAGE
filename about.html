About Us

We are a forward-thinking mobility company inspired by innovation, quality, and sustainability. Our vision is to shape the future of transportation through cutting-edge design, precision engineering, and digital intelligence.

Who We Are

As a global leader in premium mobility, we develop and deliver high-performance vehicles and intelligent services that redefine driving. Our brand family includes advanced mobility solutions and sustainable innovations driven by technology and passion.

Our Mission

To deliver premium mobility experiences that excite and inspire—today and for the generations to come. We believe in pushing boundaries, setting benchmarks, and creating a more sustainable world.

Global Impact

With a presence in over 140 countries and more than 150,000 dedicated employees worldwide, we operate over 30 production and assembly facilities across the globe. In 2024, we delivered more than 2.4 million vehicles and achieved record growth in electric mobility.

Innovation & Sustainability

From our new-generation electric vehicles to sustainable production methods, innovation drives everything we do. We are committed to responsible sourcing, reducing emissions, and preserving resources across our entire supply chain.

Driven by Values

  • Excellence – Pursuing perfection in every detail
  • Responsibility – Acting ethically and sustainably
  • Customer Centricity – Understanding and exceeding expectations
  • Innovation – Leading through smart, future-ready solutions
  • Teamwork – Empowering people across cultures and generations

Together, we move the world forward—one innovation at a time.
